FAQs

How to order from W2C?

Click on Call us to order, you will be propmted to the login page, where after you login you will receive the phone number for the store

What if I dont receive my order?

W2C only parterns with the stores who are capable of taking orders and delivering them. However, in case you haven't received your order. Please contact us on via "Contact Us" link on the page, we will try to resolve your issues with utmost priority.

How to make payments for the orders?

To make a payment for your orders, you need to choose the payment methords suitable for the store. Most stores at W2C accept all forms of payment methods. There is no connection of W2C to the stores in terms of payments.
